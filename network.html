Style research carried forward

What the recent repos taught this one.

Style guide

straddie-makerspace-lab

Use plain questions, practical workshop language, 8px radii, Atkinson Hyperlegible and Nunito Sans, sequence nav, source notes and public pages before repo links.

Style guide

Sandworm-subterranean-systems

Use a generated static site, source-aware builders, page-specific hero images, dropdown nav groups, site map and careful boundary language for speculative infrastructure.

Style guide

dunwich-gumpi-ferry-terminal-open-data-lab

Make the ferry terminal a live civic lab: official trail, open data, public photos, simulation workflow and evidence before claims.

Style guide

stradbroke-grants-lab

Connect project ideas to grant windows, profiles, checklists, milestones and reporting, not just inspiration.

Style guide

moreton-bay-community-wealth-and-mutuals

Frame wealth and risk as local stewardship questions: who holds value, who carries risk and how do receipts stay inspectable?

Style guide

ready-set-co-op-trust-hub

Keep trust, training, media and voluntary pathways clear. Avoid treating people as labour inventory.
