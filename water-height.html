Water is not spare hardware

Could pumped-hydro curiosity protect the lakes by staying digital first?

Pumped hydro normally stores energy by moving water between high and low reservoirs and generating through turbines. Straddie's perched lakes, wetlands, springs and protected areas should be treated as boundaries first.

  1. Model height, do not name a lake. Start with maps, tanks, digital twins or off-river closed loops.

  2. Protect water quality and Country. Sensitive lakes are not blank assets waiting for a clever use.

  3. Ask what else gives the same benefit. Sand heat, batteries, demand shifting or mainland-linked storage may answer the need with less risk.

The best pumped-hydro page might be the one that proves which places should never be touched.
