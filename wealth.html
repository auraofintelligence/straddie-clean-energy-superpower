Community Wealth And Mutual Care

Could clean energy income, lower bills, mutual protection and grant readiness become patient local wealth rather than another extraction story?

Community wealth

Could clean energy become shared resilience, not just cheaper bills?

The wealth question is simple: who pays, who benefits, who holds the risk, who owns the data, and who gets to say no before the island is locked into a bad deal?

  1. Lower bills first. Household and small-business wins matter.

  2. Shared assets second. Community batteries, thermal stores or emergency nodes need clear ownership and maintenance.

  3. Patient wealth grows from trust. A fund, co-op or mutual reserve can show law, governance, receipts and consent before asking people to believe in it.

A clean-energy superpower is not just a place that makes energy. It is a place that keeps the upside close enough to govern.
