- `straddie-makerspace-lab` - Use plain questions, practical workshop language, 8px radii, Atkinson Hyperlegible and Nunito Sans, sequence nav, source notes and public pages before repo links.
- `Sandworm-subterranean-systems` - Use a generated static site, source-aware builders, page-specific hero images, dropdown nav groups, site map and careful boundary language for speculative infrastructure.

- `moreton-bay-community-wealth-and-mutuals` - Frame wealth and risk as local stewardship questions: who holds value, who carries risk and how do receipts stay inspectable?
- `ready-set-co-op-trust-hub` - Keep trust, training, media and voluntary pathways clear. Do not treat people as labour inventory.

## Public Sources
- [Dunwich / Gumpi Ferry Terminal Upgrade](https://www.yoursay-projects.tmr.qld.gov.au/dunwich-gumpi-ferry-terminal-upgrade) - Queensland Department of Transport and Main Roads: Grounds the ferry gateway as a real public infrastructure node before any energy lab claim gets too abstract.
- [Small-scale installation postcode data](https://cer.gov.au/markets/reports-and-data/small-scale-installation-postcode-data) - Clean Energy Regulator: Provides the public data trail for rooftop solar and newer solar-battery postcode checks.
- [Solar power for your home](https://www.qld.gov.au/housing/buying-owning-home/energy-water-home/solar) - Queensland Government: Keeps home solar and battery guidance tied to practical household questions and safety.
- [Concentrated solar thermal](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/concentrated-solar-thermal/) - ARENA: Supports solar heat and thermal storage as a serious option, while keeping scale and cost questions visible.
- [Advanced solar thermal energy storage technologies](https://arena.gov.au/projects/advanced-solar-thermal-energy-storage-technologies/) - ARENA / CSIRO: Shows that high-temperature solar thermal storage has an Australian research and demonstration trail.
- [Thermal Energy Storage Technology Strategy Assessment](https://www.energy.gov/sites/default/files/2023-07/Technology%20Strategy%20Assessment%20-%20Thermal%20Energy%20Storage_0.pdf) - U.S. Department of Energy: Supports sand, rock and other earth-based heat storage as a research-backed long-duration storage lane.
- [Sand Battery](https://polarnightenergy.com/sand-battery/) - Polar Night Energy: Provides a plain commercial reference for sand batteries as thermal storage, not magic electric batteries.
- [Broken Hill Advanced Compressed Air Energy Storage Demonstration](https://arena.gov.au/projects/hydrostor-broken-hill-advanced-compressed-air-energy-storage-demonstration/) - ARENA: Shows compressed air as a serious long-duration storage pathway when geology, safety and economics line up.
- [Technical feasibility of CAES in an aquifer storage vessel](https://www.sandia.gov/files/ess/EESAT/2009_papers/Technical%20Feasibility%20of%20Compressed-Air%20Energy%20Storage%20in%20an%20Aquifer%20Storage%20Vessel.pdf) - Sandia / Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory authors: Keeps aquifer compressed-air language cautious because natural aquifer storage is difficult and unproven commercially.
- [Pumped Hydro Energy Storage](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/pumped-hydro-energy-storage/) - ARENA: Clarifies pumped hydro as water-height storage that normally uses turbines, reservoirs and careful siting.
- [Ocean energy](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/ocean/) - ARENA: Grounds wave and tidal energy as a broad family of technologies, not one settled device type.
- [Australian Wave Energy Atlas](https://arena.gov.au/projects/australian-wave-energy-atlas/) - ARENA / CSIRO: Supports wave-resource mapping before any marine device or reef-edge idea gets proposed.
- [Review of wave energy converter power take-off systems](https://www.nrel.gov/docs/fy23osti/82807.pdf) - NREL: Adds a research trail for non-standard wave power take-off concepts, including non-air-turbine pathways.
- [Carbon capture and storage](https://www.ga.gov.au/aecr2024/carbon-capture-and-storage) - Geoscience Australia: Frames carbon capture as capture, transport, compression and storage, not a casual add-on to local air tanks.
- [Carbon dioxide as a fire suppressant: examining the risks](https://www.epa.gov/snap/carbon-dioxide-fire-suppressant-examining-risks) - US EPA: Keeps CO2 fire-suppression ideas behind strong life-safety controls and occupied-space warnings.
- [Naree Budjong Djara National Park](https://parks.qld.gov.au/parks/naree-budjong-djara) - Queensland Parks: Keeps protected-area, lake, wetland and Country boundaries visible before infrastructure imagination runs ahead.
- [Quandamooka Yoolooburrabee Aboriginal Corporation](https://qyac.net.au/) - QYAC: Names the public organisational doorway for Quandamooka land and sea management rather than implying authority.
