# Source Notes

This note records the public sources and nearby repos used for the first clean-energy atlas.

## Local Working Inputs
- `Straddie_Sovereign_Wealth_Fund_&_Civilisation_of_Sand_Research_Brief.md` - Working concept fuel only. Adds wave and tidal options, solar resource framing, sand batteries, vanadium flow batteries, salt-gradient questions, oyster-crete reefs, sovereign wealth fund architecture, token questions and a source-trail habit.

## Style And Repo Research
- `straddie-makerspace-lab` - Use plain questions, practical workshop language, 8px radii, Atkinson Hyperlegible and Nunito Sans, sequence nav, source notes and public pages before repo links.
- `Sandworm-subterranean-systems` - Use a generated static site, source-aware builders, page-specific hero images, dropdown nav groups, site map and careful boundary language for speculative infrastructure.
- `dunwich-gumpi-ferry-terminal-open-data-lab` - Make the ferry terminal a live civic lab: official trail, open data, public photos, simulation workflow and evidence before claims.
- `stradbroke-grants-lab` - Connect project ideas to grant windows, profiles, checklists, milestones and reporting, not just inspiration.
- `moreton-bay-community-wealth-and-mutuals` - Frame wealth and risk as local stewardship questions: who holds value, who carries risk and how do receipts stay inspectable?
- `ready-set-co-op-trust-hub` - Keep trust, training, media and voluntary pathways clear. Avoid treating people as labour inventory.

## Public Sources
- [Dunwich / Gumpi Ferry Terminal Upgrade](https://www.yoursay-projects.tmr.qld.gov.au/dunwich-gumpi-ferry-terminal-upgrade) - Queensland Department of Transport and Main Roads: Grounds the ferry gateway as a real public infrastructure node before any energy lab claim gets too abstract.
- [Small-scale installation postcode data](https://cer.gov.au/markets/reports-and-data/small-scale-installation-postcode-data) - Clean Energy Regulator: Provides the public data trail for rooftop solar and newer solar-battery postcode checks.
- [Solar power for your home](https://www.qld.gov.au/housing/buying-owning-home/energy-water-home/solar) - Queensland Government: Keeps home solar and battery guidance tied to practical household, electrical and network questions.
- [Feed-in tariffs](https://www.qld.gov.au/housing/buying-owning-home/energy-water-home/solar/feed-in-tariffs) - Queensland Government: Clarifies that South East Queensland feed-in tariffs are market offers and that regional customers have a separate regulated pathway.
- [Solar feed-in tariffs](https://www.ergon.com.au/retail/residential/tariffs-and-prices/solar-feed-in-tariff) - Ergon Energy Retail: Provides a current Queensland reference point for why exported solar value can be much lower than the value of useful local consumption.
- [Community Batteries for Household Solar program](https://www.dcceew.gov.au/energy/renewable/community-batteries) - Australian Government DCCEEW: Grounds neighbourhood batteries as shared storage that can lower bills, store excess solar, reduce grid pressure and help households without their own solar.
- [Community batteries](https://www.ergon.com.au/network/manage-your-energy/smarter-energy/our-network-batteries/community-batteries) - Ergon Energy Network: Shows Queensland network batteries storing rooftop solar locally and being tested through Battery Neighbourhoods.
- [Unlocking CER Benefits Through Flexible Trading rule change](https://energyinnovationtoolkit.gov.au/article/regulatory-changes/unlocking-cer-benefits-through-flexible-trading-rule-change-final) - AER Energy Innovation Toolkit: Frames flexible trading, consumer energy resources, EV batteries, smart meters and multiple service providers as an emerging regulated pathway.
- [Virtual Power Plant demonstrations](https://www.aemo.com.au/initiatives/major-programs/nem-distributed-energy-resources-der-program/der-demonstrations/virtual-power-plant-vpp-demonstrations) - AEMO: Defines virtual power plants as coordinated solar, storage and controllable loads that can provide value to customers and the energy system.
- [Virtual Power Plant regulatory use case](https://energyinnovationtoolkit.gov.au/regulatory-use-case-aggregation-distributed-energy-resources-through-virtual-power-plant) - AER Energy Innovation Toolkit: Keeps VPP, community battery and network support revenue language tied to consumer obligations, telemetry, distributor agreements and uncertainty.
- [Queensland's Electric Vehicle Super Highway](https://www.tmr.qld.gov.au/community-and-environment/electric-vehicles/queenslands-electric-super-highway) - Queensland Department of Transport and Main Roads: Connects island EV-charging questions to Queensland's public fast-charging network and transport planning context.
- [Powerledger](https://powerledger.io/) - Powerledger: Provides an industry reference for blockchain-based tracking, tracing and trading of renewable energy.
- [Everything you want to know about peer-to-peer energy trading](https://powerledger.io/media/everything-you-want-to-know-about-peer-to-peer-energy-trading/) - Powerledger: Supplies a non-government example of peer-to-peer energy trading claims, useful only as a prompt for questions about retailer, utility, price and consumer-law design.
- [Concentrated solar thermal](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/concentrated-solar-thermal/) - ARENA: Supports solar heat and thermal storage as a serious option, while keeping scale and cost questions visible.
- [Advanced solar thermal energy storage technologies](https://arena.gov.au/projects/advanced-solar-thermal-energy-storage-technologies/) - ARENA / CSIRO: Shows that high-temperature solar thermal storage has an Australian research and demonstration trail.
- [Thermal Energy Storage Technology Strategy Assessment](https://www.energy.gov/sites/default/files/2023-07/Technology%20Strategy%20Assessment%20-%20Thermal%20Energy%20Storage_0.pdf) - U.S. Department of Energy: Supports sand, rock and other earth-based heat storage as a research-backed long-duration storage lane.
- [Sand Battery](https://polarnightenergy.com/sand-battery/) - Polar Night Energy: Provides a plain commercial reference for sand batteries as thermal storage, not magic electric batteries.
- [Australia's National Hydrogen Strategy 2024](https://www.dcceew.gov.au/energy/publications/australias-national-hydrogen-strategy) - Australian Government DCCEEW: Sets the national green-hydrogen context: production, use, export, community benefit and infrastructure questions.
- [Hydrogen](https://www.csiro.au/en/research/environmental-impacts/fuels/hydrogen) - CSIRO: Provides a plain science reference for hydrogen production, storage, transport, use and Australia's hydrogen opportunity.
- [Renewable hydrogen](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/hydrogen/) - ARENA: Grounds hydrogen as renewable electricity plus water electrolysis, while keeping cost, scale and end-use questions visible.
- [Beneficial reuse and disposal options for brine in Queensland](https://gisera.csiro.au/beneficial-reuse-and-disposal-options-for-brine-in-queensland/) - CSIRO GISERA: Opens desalination brine as a resource-recovery and reuse question rather than a waste afterthought.
- [Mining valuable minerals from seawater: a critical review](https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2017/ew/c6ew00268d) - Environmental Science: Water Research & Technology / RSC: Adds a research trail for seawater and brine mineral recovery, including lithium, magnesium and other dissolved resources.
- [South East Queensland Report Card](https://reportcard.hlw.org.au/) - Healthy Land & Water: Gives the Moreton Bay water-quality context for asking what clean-energy wealth, sensors and brine research could help repair.
- [South East Queensland report card - Purpose](https://wetlandinfo.des.qld.gov.au/wetlands/facts-maps/report-card-organisation-healthy-land-and-water/) - Queensland WetlandInfo: Frames the official report-card purpose: tracking waterway health, pollutant loads, ecosystem condition and progress over time.
- [Broken Hill Advanced Compressed Air Energy Storage Demonstration](https://arena.gov.au/projects/hydrostor-broken-hill-advanced-compressed-air-energy-storage-demonstration/) - ARENA: Shows compressed air as a serious long-duration storage pathway when geology, pressure behaviour and economics line up.
- [Technical feasibility of CAES in an aquifer storage vessel](https://www.sandia.gov/files/ess/EESAT/2009_papers/Technical%20Feasibility%20of%20Compressed-Air%20Energy%20Storage%20in%20an%20Aquifer%20Storage%20Vessel.pdf) - Sandia / Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory authors: Opens the aquifer compressed-air question as a hydrology, geology, pressure and modelling topic rather than a casual slogan.
- [Pumped Hydro Energy Storage](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/pumped-hydro-energy-storage/) - ARENA: Explains pumped hydro as a serious long-duration storage family based on moving water between higher and lower reservoirs.
- [Snowy 2.0](https://www.snowyhydro.com.au/snowy-20/about/) - Snowy Hydro: Reference system for large pumped-hydro storage: upper and lower reservoirs, tunnels, reversible generation and long-duration grid support.
- [Wivenhoe Pumped Storage Hydro Power Station](https://cleancoqueensland.com.au/portfolio/owned-and-operated/wivenhoe-pumped-storage-hydro-power-station/) - CleanCo Queensland: Queensland pumped-hydro reference: water is lifted from Wivenhoe Dam to Splityard Creek Dam, then released for peak support.
- [250MW Kidston Pumped Storage Hydro Project](https://genexpower.com.au/250mw-kidston-pumped-storage-hydro-project/) - Genex Power: Queensland reference for pairing pumped storage with renewable generation and long-duration evening supply.
- [Borumba Pumped Hydro Project](https://qldhydro.com.au/projects/borumba/) - Queensland Hydro: Queensland reference for proposed long-duration pumped hydro and the scale of planning behind new water-height storage.
- [Global Greenfield Pumped Hydro Energy Storage Atlas](https://re100.eng.anu.edu.au/global/) - ANU RE100 Group: Shows how pumped-hydro thinking can begin as open topographic screening before local informal ideas choose their own shape.
- [Coastal and subcoastal non-floodplain sand lake - Perched hydrology](https://wetlandinfo.detsi.qld.gov.au/wetlands/ecology/aquatic-ecosystems-natural/lacustrine/non-floodplain-perched-lake/hydrology.html) - Queensland WetlandInfo: Grounds Straddie-style water thinking in sandmass hydrology: rapid infiltration, little overland run-off, local groundwater systems and perched lakes.
- [Saving Straddie's Sand](https://science.nasa.gov/earth/earth-observatory/saving-straddies-sand-146669/) - NASA Earth Observatory: Adds a remote-sensing source for Straddie's sandmass, mineral-sands history and landform questions.
- [Vortex Bladeless](https://vortexbladeless.com/) - Vortex Bladeless: Opens vortex-induced oscillation as one no-blade wind family for local energy-harvesting imagination.
- [The motionless wind energy system](https://aerominetechnologies.com/) - Aeromine Technologies: Shows rooftop edge-flow wind harvesting as a real commercial research and product pathway.
- [EWICON](https://www.mecanoo.nl/projects/project/61/ewicon) - Mecanoo / Delft project: Adds electrostatic wind conversion without moving mechanical parts to the no-blade wind source trail.
- [Low Wind Energy](https://smartshelterfoundation.org/collection/low-wind-energy) - Smart Shelter Foundation: Provides a plain Windbelt-style doorway into low-velocity ribbon and flutter harvesting.
- [Vortex-induced vibration wind energy harvesting by piezoelectric MEMS device in formation](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-019-56786-0) - Scientific Reports: Supports formation-scale micro-harvesters and the idea that many small wind motions can be studied together.
- [Recent progress on flutter-based wind energy harvesting](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/msd2.12035) - Wiley: Supports flutter-based wind harvesting as a research family for microelectronics and small devices.
- [Structural design strategies of triboelectric nanogenerators for wind energy harvesting](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s40486-025-00224-6) - Springer: Adds wind-driven fluttering triboelectric films to the fractal wind-harvesting source trail.
- [Harvesting ambient wind energy with an inverted piezoelectric flag](https://pure.johnshopkins.edu/en/publications/harvesting-ambient-wind-energy-with-an-inverted-piezoelectric-fla) - Johns Hopkins / Applied Physics Letters: Shows how flag geometry and aeroelastic flutter can turn low-speed wind into small electric output.
- [Ocean energy](https://arena.gov.au/renewable-energy/ocean/) - ARENA: Grounds wave and tidal energy as a broad family of technologies, not one settled device type.
- [Marine Energy Basics](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/water/marine-energy-basics) - U.S. Department of Energy: Keeps marine-energy language broad: waves, tides, currents, temperature gradients and many conversion pathways.
- [Australian Wave Energy Atlas](https://arena.gov.au/projects/australian-wave-energy-atlas/) - ARENA / CSIRO: Supports wave-resource mapping as one doorway into the many unmapped wave and tide possibilities.
- [Review of wave energy converter power take-off systems](https://www.nrel.gov/docs/fy23osti/82807.pdf) - NREL: Adds a research trail for non-standard wave power take-off concepts, including non-air-turbine pathways.
- [Vortex Hydro Energy develops transformational technology](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/success-stories/articles/eere-success-story-vortex-hydro-energy-develops-transformational) - U.S. Department of Energy: Reference for vortex-induced vibration as a no-blade current-energy pathway inspired by how fish interact with vortices.
- [Energy harvesting by flow-induced flutter in an inverted piezoelectric flag](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/journal-of-fluid-mechanics/article/abs/energy-harvesting-by-flowinduced-flutter-in-a-simple-model-of-an-inverted-piezoelectric-flag/37FF699E5B55E238A41F056E4A06DBDB) - Journal of Fluid Mechanics: Supports flutter and flexible-geometry energy harvesting as a real first-principles research lane.
- [Analysis of biomimetic stream energy device based on flapping foils](https://tethys-engineering.pnnl.gov/publications/analysis-biomimetic-stream-energy-device-based-flapping-foils) - Tethys Engineering / PNNL: Reference for biomimetic oscillating foils as a hydrokinetic design family beyond standard turbines.
- [Shrimp metachronal swimming](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10303694/) - National Library of Medicine / open-access article: Opens shrimp-style appendage timing and cascading motion as geometry clues, not finished energy claims.
- [Mechanics of Movement: Mantis Shrimp](https://pateklab.biology.duke.edu/research/mechanics-of-ultrafast-movement/mechanics-of-movement-mantis-shrimp/) - Patek Lab, Duke University: Gives a public biology reference for latch, spring and cavitation ideas that can inspire playful marine-energy questions.
- [Carbon capture and storage](https://www.ga.gov.au/aecr2024/carbon-capture-and-storage) - Geoscience Australia: Frames carbon capture as capture, transport, compression and storage, not a casual add-on to local air tanks.
- [Carbon dioxide as a fire suppressant: examining the risks](https://www.epa.gov/snap/carbon-dioxide-fire-suppressant-examining-risks) - US EPA: Adds an authority source for CO2 fire-suppression questions, especially where people may occupy the space.
- [Naree Budjong Djara National Park](https://parks.qld.gov.au/parks/naree-budjong-djara) - Queensland Parks: Keeps protected-area, lake, wetland and Country boundaries visible before infrastructure imagination runs ahead.
- [Quandamooka Yoolooburrabee Aboriginal Corporation](https://qyac.net.au/) - QYAC: Names the public organisational doorway for Quandamooka land and sea management rather than implying authority.
